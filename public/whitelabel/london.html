EXAMPLE ITINERARY

Sunday - Arrive in London, England
Arrive at Heathrow airport, be collected by private bus for the short transfer to your London accommodation. Settle in, make some calls home, then grab dinner.

Monday - Central London
See the sights with a local guide on the
Top 10 London Sights Walking Tour.
Lunch in Central London then visit to the
Natural History Museum.

Tuesday: University and Chelsea FC
Visit Imperial College London, discover what life
is like at a leading UK university. After lunch,
ride the Tube across central London to
Chelsea Football Club for the Official Stadium Tour.

Wednesday: Oxford Street & Chinatown
Vist Europe’s longest shopping street, Oxford Street, for some serious retail therapy. The rest of the day you are free to do as you please. In the evening, explore Chinatown, and enjoy a 12-course Chinese banquet!!

Thursday: Windsor Castle
Visit the Royal Family’s home, Windsor Castle. Then there’s free time to explore more. Return to London in time for dinner.

Friday: Camden Market & West End
In the morning visit Camden Market - grab gifts for family and friends and find something to wear for our big night out tonight! Friday night is movie night. Get dressed up for a night in Leicester Square at one of London’s top cinemas.

Saturday: Leave London, England
It’s time to say farewell to London. After breakfast, travel back to Heathrow Airport by private mini coach for your flight home.
